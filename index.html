Our Mission

Accountability Where It Has Been Absent

The commercial maritime industry operates under a federal credentialing framework administered by the United States Coast Guard National Maritime Center. While that framework establishes rigorous standards for mariner licensing and credential oversight, independent research has identified areas where systemic gaps may exist. Maritime Credential Integrity Group LLC was founded to examine those gaps, document findings through verified evidence, and offer constructive assistance toward strengthening the integrity of the nation's maritime credentialing system.

About the Organization

Founded by a Credentialed Mariner and Former Law Enforcement Professional

The organization's work draws on direct experience with both the maritime credentialing system and large-scale cross-database statistical analysis developed during law enforcement service.
